The Human Element

As stunning as the estate is in both its location and construction in order to complete the entire project with excellence the human element needed to also be on par with the first two & we are very pleased to say that has been achieved if not surpassed.

Our Staff have intrinsically recognized the level of quality of the residence and the uniqueness of the location and have over time elevated themselves to a level of discrete presence and performance that will leave the most discerning of guests comfortably pleased.

Ever willing to cater to your every need yet hardly visible when not required you will enjoy an experience similar to a quality first class flight. The staff takes great pride in providing their services all carefully supervised by the watchful eye of the Butler.

The spa therapists are already well trained and experienced from overseas work experience in the Mediterranean then elevated again by Asia's highest spa training service to the highest standards for the Luxe Bali in-house Spa.

Our very talented and creative Chef, will delight you with meals prepared from our comprehensive menu or take great delight in making any dish you may request that is not on the menu should the ingredients be available in Bali.

While you are here we strongly urge you to sample the wonderful delights of Indonesian cooking made from the freshest locally sourced ingredients, it will be an experience well remembered.

Please see with the Guest Comments section of this website and note how often and how profoundly the guests are touched by the staff, the service and the meals. This speaks more than anything we can possibly say.

One final note, being Bali there will always be that special Bali smile and friendliness that no amount of formality will ever completely erase in our staff, and this we celebrate and feel sure that you will also.
