Residence Details

The Environment

The Environment: Extensive planning in design engineering was focused on energy efficiency, water efficiency and environmental considerations. Energy efficient high quality dimmable LED lighting is used throughout the property to reduce electrical demand. A fully integrated Vantage USA Smart Home Control system is used for all Lighting -Music - Security and CCTV, The system is connected to sensors that Turn on and off lights when entering/ leaving a space.

Power management soft-wear records power consumption every minute and transmits 19 variables including Per phase Volts, Amps, Watts, Power Factors, to a cloud website where The Luxe can monitor and make decisions to achieve more efficient power management.

The large floor-to-ceiling custom glass doors and very effective cross ventilation minimize the need for air conditioning reducing the electrical demand. Should you require a cooler space air conditioning is possible in every area.

Should there be a power outage a large 115 KV on-site backup generator automatically switches on in four seconds to maintain a continuous supply of electricity to the residents.

Most building and furniture coatings use biodegradable earth friendly and high durability products from Sweden and the USA.

The property also includes 500,000m3 of underground tanks for holding pure rainwater captured and used within the property. Custom fresh water filtration and activation systems process the water from the tanks to end up with house use water higher in purity than most bottled drinking water. Taking this further for the upmost quality in drinking water, the water is further reverse osmosis filtered then passed through alkaline filter media to arrive at a healthy PH8 with no contaminants at all.

The wastewater from the house is processed in a very advanced 60m3 bio system that ends up with clear, odorless, virus and bacteria free water specifically for gardening purposes. All organic food and garden waste is composted and returned to the soil , and all plastic and paper is collected for recycling by Eco Bali. These are responsibilities we take seriously.
