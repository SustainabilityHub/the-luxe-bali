Residence Details

Spa, Gym and Home Theater

Spa, Gym and Home Theater: These luxuries are all found on level one of the residence. This level also provides access to the large cliff side infinity pool, covered pergola's, and garden pathway that winds down to your direct access to the Indian Ocean.

There is a very spacious air-conditioned fitness - yoga centre which includes gym equipment and yoga/martial arts mats, with a large wall mounted flat screen TV and Hi-Fi sound system.

A full sized sauna clad with aromatic Canadian redwood cedar and a large travertine lined steam room make for a wonderfully relaxing experience. Then onto the in-house professional spa and beauty area with two massage tables complete with comprehensive body and beauty treatment equipment. Adjoining the massage area is a peaceful manicure-pedicure & reflexology area with views and access to the main cliff edge pool and outdoor pavilion.

A dedicated home theater room is likely to be the highlight of the first floor, it is equipped with plush reclining leather lounge chairs, a state-of-the-art HD projector with 3-meter wide screen with Blue Ray movie player and a state of the art 7.1 surround sound system to provide a movie experience comparable to the best upmarket theaters worldwide.

Regardless of what the weather may be outside, there is always at the ready an entire health & entertainment set up ready for you to enjoy indoors.
