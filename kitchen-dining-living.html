Residence Details

Kitchen – Dining – Living

Kitchen: The main kitchen is equipped with a top of the line professional Wolf cooking range, Sub Zero fridge freezer, and a wine storage unit. Ample benches and an center island workspace provide a comfortable work area with storage for all utensils and foodstuffs. In the pantry there is a large walk in chiller to keep all your produce clean and fresh.

Large slabs of kashmir gold granite on both the floor and work benches provide a beautiful and easy to clean workspace. The Kitchen is able to accommodate the most demanding of chefs. It was designed and fitted out with the knowledge that some high profile guests bring their own chefs with them therefore every attempt was made to provide a kitchen with the facility to enable them to perform to the best of their ability while maintaining wonderful views and settings for all.

Dining: The Main Dining area is stunning not only in it's 360 degree views of gardens, in house koi ponds, and ocean views even when all doors are closed and fully air-conditioned, but also in the breathtaking architectural features, art and artifacts that surround you.

The main rainforest and ocean view terraces both have comfortable seating & tables for breakfasts and lunches. One of the most stunning features of the Dining and Living space being the vast expanse of beautifully waxed timber flooring edged with travertine.

We have a passion for fine food, you will find the dining experience at the Luxe Bali pleasantly beyond your expectations and will wonder why the Chef is not working at a high end restaurant. Rather that talking too much about his ability please see guests comments further down this website to note how often the quality of food has been a major feature and enjoyment in the guests stay. An extensive menu of delicious Indonesian, , Contemporary Western, Asian , Japanese , Indian , Italian , Vegetarian and Children's dishes are available. Special dietary requests are possible and dinner parties can be catered for. Select any meal you desire from our extensive menu, any book or website and should the ingredients be available in Bali leave it to the Chef.

Living: The Living space occupies most of the ground floor with several seating and lounging areas each with it's own ambiance and surround art. There is a large 55" TV area where both children and adults may be entertained while remaining within view. A quiet office study lined with historic Books on Bali – Indonesia - Tropical Homes and Gardens - Novels – Art , and Personal Growth for those preferring to read is available, and a work desk with an iMac computer for your use when required.

Covered terraces paved with non slip marble contain ample lounges to provide a comfortable space to sit outdoors while relaxing, watching the surf - ocean - gardens - koi pond or pool.
