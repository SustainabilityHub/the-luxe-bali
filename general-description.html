Residence Details

General Description

The 3 bedroom Villa The Luxe Bali located above the famous Bali Padang-Padang Surf Beach on the Bukit Peninsula, is literally the height of luxury.

One of only three private residences within an exclusive gated estate covering 5,000 m2 of land, The Luxe Bali borders 26 hectares of virgin forest. Perched high on one of Bali's prime cliff top locations, the villa offers astonishing views of one of Bali's most famous surf breaks and beach below, the shoreline of Kuta and Seminyak in the distance, and of Bali's majestic sacred volcano, Mount Agung rising behind it all.

Staying at The Luxe Bali is your First Class Bali Villa experience. The Luxe Bali is one of the only cliff top properties in Bali where one may easily walk all the way down the terraced garden paths to the water's edge and dive in or go surfing in the Indian Ocean. Alternatively one can enjoy the vista from the estate's own cliff-edge pavilion.

The 1270 m2 of living space boasts three Master Suites – each with its own private terrace, lounge and en-suite bathroom. The Penthouse Suite features a stunning 88 m2 cream marble terrace - one of the premier spots in Bali from which to watch the sun set over the Indian Ocean. The King-size beds – strategically placed to catch the views – have the finest mattresses, cotton sheets and non-allergic microfiber pillows.

All four floors of The Luxe Bali are wheelchair accessible via the 6-person elevator. The villa also offers a Gym (which easily converts into a children's bedroom), Swedish Sauna, Steam Room and a Spa manned by two fully trained therapists. A climate-controlled Home Theatre features a 3 meter diagonal screen and leather reclining chairs, including a double love seat. A Smart Home System, programmed for easy, intuitive use by the Guest, controls the lighting, music, CCTV and alarm system, one can run the villa from one's chair – or bed!. Constant high water pressure micro-filtered, UV-treated, fully-drinkable hot and cold rainwater is supplied to all showers, vanities and baths and are but a few of the unique features illustrating the attention to detail which is the leitmotif of this unique property.

Other outstanding Amenities and Services include, State-of-the-Art Kitchen, equipment and a very talented chef skilled preparing international as well as authentic local cuisine. A highly trained team of staff, 24 hour butler service, Spa & Sauna facilities offering in-house massage treatments by experienced massage therapists, a white marble infinity pool, beautifully crafted custom-made wood and rattan furnishings, and HD Satellite TV.
