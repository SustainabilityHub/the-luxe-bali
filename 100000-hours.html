The Story

100,000 Hours

In the same vein as the Unlimited Budget, the residence was built without any specific deadline as it was more important for the owner to get it right than it was to finish quickly. The man hour figure spent in construction is just one more way to illustrate the incredible commitment the owner has applied to actualize the vision of creating the ultimate luxury residence in Bali.

The amount of thought – research and application by the owner – the architect - the German master builder – the smart home installer and programmers – the sound engineers - the air conditioning and plumbing engineers, the cabinet makers – the custom made furniture company – the stonemasons – the gardeners and garden planning – the living water consultants – the lighting engineer & fixture designer – the artists – and the team of interior designers working on the one residence were on par with the worlds best.

Overall a phenomenal amount of time and energy was spent as everyone fell into the enchantment of pursuing excellence, all involved seeking the finished quality of their work to exceed the quality of the others involved in the project. If you appreciate fine craftsmanship, excellent application of the finest technologies, exceptional quality equipment, and precision engineering merged into one masterpiece you will be in awe with the residence.

Most of the technical applications are hidden in the background discretely operating to give you a seamless most comfortable stay, similar to flying in a first class suite. This is The Luxe Bali, we invite you to experience another level of excellence in accommodation.
