The Story

World-Class Materials

The commitment to creating the finest residence in Bali can be seen in the quality and the scope of the sources used for the materials in this exceptional residence. Extensive use of the finest local timbers and stone protected with the highest quality environmentally friendly coatings from Sweden and the USA. A significant quantity of the custom-made furnishings and decorations within the residence come from the finest artisans and craftsmen amongst the exotic islands in Indonesia and tastefully compliments the stately nature of the property.

Added to this is an array of functional products from Italy, Germany, Australia, and USA and beyond. For example the travertine stone used both for flooring and vertical wall surfaces was obtained from Turkey, the quality sound system components from the USA and England. The outer skin of the building is fully clad in high quality light grey honed granite. A high end Vantage USA smart home system connected to art gallery quality dimmable LED lighting provide a comfortable ambiance. King Koil Masterpiece mattresses, Sheraton quality pillows and highest quality Frette woven Italian bed sheets, Grohe and Kohler pressure and temperature control plumbing fixtures & oversized bath taps able to fill a large bath in 3 minutes, a professional grade Wolf cooking range and SubZero freezer refrigerator, are all the finest that money can buy and a sublime pleasure to use, and lets not forget the walk in refrigerated cold room in the pantry that keeps all produce safe & fresh for the maximum enjoyment of your meals.

A significantly amount of the art used in the residence was created by long standing International artists Davina Stephens (http://davina0.com) and Carlos de Goyeneche both well known internationally for their work and who have created stunning pieces for the residence. Several of the major pieces were produced over time to compliment the house in situ with the surrounding views, the end result being that the art and the residence fit perfectly together, and many an hour can be spent sitting with each piece. These unique pieces compliment an extensive collection of Indonesian Archipelago arts and crafts assembled over 40 years.
