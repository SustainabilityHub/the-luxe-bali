The Story

Priceless Location

It is no exaggeration that Bali's most breathtaking ocean views are offered at certain locations on the Bukit, The Luxe Bali is located at one of these handful of unique locations which is on the tip of the Padang Padang, Uluwatu Peninsula and offers the rare opportunity to look across (and into) the most beautiful section of ocean in Bali. The Islands Volcano's and mountains are visible across the vast bay during the mornings while the lights of Jimbaran, Kuta, Legian, and Seminyuk are visible at night.

The upper portion of the property sits at an elevation of the about 40 meters and offers "to die for views" into the water and revealing dazzling arrays of blue and turquoise across the bay and the cliffs to the side. Gradually terraced stairs lead from the upper space down to the lower portion of the property that is actually at water level providing private access for swimming and surfing. Most ocean front property in Bali offers either elevated views or beachfront access, The Luxe Bali offers both.
