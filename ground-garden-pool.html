Residence Details

Grounds, Gardens and Pools

Grounds Gardens and Pools: As the residence cascades from the roadway down to the water level 40 meters below you will discover numerous exterior areas for relaxing and taking in the amazing views. Mature lush gardens abound within the estate. Leading down the tree canopy covered driveway past a 26 meter lap pool to the Luxe Bali private entrance. Walking down the stairs or taking the elevator one arrives at an enchanted "rainforest garden" located in front of the main foyer and breakfast terrace of the Luxe Bali Residence. This garden includes a natural koi pond with unique flow form waterfalls, which provide the relaxing sounds of a natural stream as the water flows into the koi pond. Another separate flow form waterfall activates & oxygenates the pure rainwater for house use after extensive filtering. Lush Gardens surround the living level (level 2) on all four sides, the ocean side of the living level also includes a large private garden, and grassed area that extends to the edge of the cliff overlooking the surf below.

One level down on the gym - spa - home theatre level (level one) you have full access to the large outdoor function area, which includes a 20 meter long cliff edge infinity pool and large covered terrace with relaxing day beds that overlook the surf and beaches below. All external furniture uses quality weatherproof fabric and quick dry foam cushions for the highest comfort level.

Cascading stairs fringed with lush vegetation descend from level one past the cliff edge infinity pool down past a lower cliff edged gazebo where watching the surf or the activities of Padang2 Beach are possible , then onwards all the way down the side of the cliff to the waters edge to provide private access to the ocean for swimming, surfing, and or walking along the rock pools and beach at low tide.

The lower cliff edge gazebo provides a private place for morning coffee or reading along with the accompanying sounds of the surf below. The Luxe Bali features its own private cool water white marble pool adjoining the main level terrace and shaded by a magnificent banyun tree. The pool features adjustable color pool lights to create any desired mood in the evening.

Koi Ponds. The Luxe has two crystal clear koi ponds, one in the breakfast terrace rainforest garden and one adjoining the dining area containing large healthy koi. The lush gardens within the property are modelled on an Ubud forrest grotto.
