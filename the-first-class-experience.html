The Story

The First Class Experience

When building the residence, the owners were very aware of the business/executive class style of marketing luxury villas in Bali that offers many bedrooms in order to entice a large group to collectively obtain 'a bargain’. Rather than fitting in 4 up to 7 bedrooms into the residence the owners consciously selected to include within the same space a more intimate number of expansive and luxurious bedroom suites ( 3 ) filled with fine art and exceptional facilities for the pleasure of a discerning few.

The Luxe Bali owners being frequent first class flight travellers on the worlds best airlines and hotels fully understand the difference between business and first class and wished to create the intimate ambiance, facility, and services in a residence that one who chooses first class would already know and appreciate. For example our bathing and showering water is as clean, safe, and pure as bottled drinking water. While our actual drinking water is taken to another level to become the finest filtered alkaline friendly drinking water available.

We most respectfully seek and invite guests that understand the difference and consider what is the level of comfort, facility, and service they are able to enjoy during the stay as their priority criteria when choosing a residence. For these guests we will make every effort to make their stay as wonderful and memorable as possible. All of us at The Luxe Bali are also very happy to receive quests that are not frequent first class travellers and take the upmost pleasure in providing for them a memorable experience that we feel will last a lifetime.

Guests that wish to travel alone, as a couple, or with a small family The Luxe Bali also offers the rental of the entire residence in 1 and 2 bedroom options, priced accordingly.
