Residence Details

Bedrooms & Bathrooms

Bedrooms: The bedroom suites located on level three and four are stunningly luxurious & spacious and include in room lounging areas and large en-suite bathrooms

Each Bedroom is tastefully complete with fine art, archipelago artifacts and excellent furnishings. Stunning ocean and garden views can be enjoyed via extra large floor to ceiling Italian sliding glass doors with 1cm thick tempered glass creating absolute silence within the room. Draw the curtains fully open and go to bed while watching the sparkling lights of fishing boats and Seminyak across the bay.

The King size beds use the highest quality available Masterpiece mattresses with Frette fine Italian sheets & covers and Sheraton quality pillows. Some guests have commented that they have never slept so well.

Each bedroom also includes it's own private open terrace with seating areas for enjoying the rainforest garden view or the stunning ocean views.

Large rotating 46" HD TV's connected to both Satellite and Cable plus DVD players keep you entertained with movies – sports and the morning news either In bed or in the accompanying lounge areas.

The lounges and coffee tables invite you to spend a day in your Suite without needing to come down should you so desire.

Bathrooms: The floor to ceiling travertine-lined bathrooms are perhaps the most comfortable and visually stunning in Bali, they certainly have every luxury thought of and provided. Faucets equipped with Grohe constant pressure & temperature controlled showers and oversized Kohler ceramic valve faucets for rapidly filling the baths.

The Penthouse Suite and the Ocean View Suite have large jacuzzi's while the Rainforest suite has a deep bathtub. Your bath will fill in under 4 minutes so you will never need to waste time before bathing and for showers the hot water is immediate throughout the building. A convenient space for your drink or book is provided surrounding each bathtub and a high quality focused reading light is overhead should you wish to read. All bathrooms have either garden or ocean views. The Bedrooms and the Bathrooms are of the highest level in materials and style available anywhere in Bali.
